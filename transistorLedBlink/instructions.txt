Instructions
1. You can use any resistor values above 270Ω instead of the 330Ω resistors.
2. You can use any resistors between 1k and 10k instead of the 10k resistors.
3. You can use both polarized and non polarized capacitors.
4. Changing th capacitor or the last connected resistor values will change the blink duration of the LEDs.
5. If you use any other transistors than the BC547, then check thier pinouts and connect them according to the circuit diagrams.
6. Go through the circuit diagrams in order. That is first you create the circuit in step1.png, then step2.png, step3.png etc....
